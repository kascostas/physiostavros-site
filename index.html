About the Clinic

Physio Stavros Stavrou provides focused, one-to-one physiotherapy care in Strovolos. The approach combines detailed assessment, clear treatment planning, and realistic goals that fit your lifestyle.

Each session is designed to restore function, improve confidence in movement, and prevent recurrence.

Who we help

  • Office workers with neck and back pain
  • Active adults recovering from injuries
  • People managing chronic pain and stiffness
  • Patients rebuilding strength after downtime
